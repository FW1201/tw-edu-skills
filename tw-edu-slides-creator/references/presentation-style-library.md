# Presentation Style Library

Use these as style systems, not rigid templates. Keep one visual identity across the deck while varying layouts by teaching purpose.

## Edu Warm Classroom

- Best for: general K-12 lessons, workshops, classroom examples.
- Palette: warm ivory, clay orange, soft blue, muted green.
- Typography: friendly Traditional Chinese sans, large body text.
- Layout: example cards, activity panels, step flows, guided practice.
- Avoid: childish clip art, emoji decoration, tiny text, dense tables.

## Academic Clean

- Best for: high-school content, literature analysis, research-based lessons.
- Palette: off-white, graphite, calm blue, muted rust.
- Typography: sober headings, readable captions.
- Layout: claim-evidence-analysis, comparison tables, annotated source excerpts.
- Avoid: decorative illustrations unrelated to evidence.

## Neon Circuit

- Best for: AI literacy, coding, digital learning, platform workflows.
- Palette: dark navy, cyan, violet, restrained orange accent.
- Typography: sharp headings with readable Traditional Chinese body text.
- Layout: workflow diagrams, system maps, modular cards.
- Avoid: excessive glow, tiny code, unreadable neon contrast.

## Hand-drawn Explainer

- Best for: abstract concepts, grammar, process, rhetorical structure.
- Palette: paper white, ink black, one or two marker accents.
- Typography: hand-drawn only if legible; otherwise clean sans.
- Layout: annotated diagrams, arrows, simple metaphor sketches.
- Avoid: messy handwriting, garbled Chinese, excessive doodles.

## Local Culture

- Best for: Chinese literature, Taiwan culture, local history, humanities.
- Palette: rice paper, ink, muted red, tea green, dark brown.
- Typography: editorial Traditional Chinese hierarchy.
- Layout: text excerpt plus annotation, timeline, character relationship map.
- Avoid: fake antique texture that reduces readability.

## Workshop Dashboard

- Best for: teacher PD, administrative training, tool workflows.
- Palette: light dashboard base, crisp accent colors, clear status colors.
- Typography: compact headings, large process labels.
- Layout: agenda, workflow, checkpoint, action list, exit ticket.
- Avoid: fake metrics, chart junk, unexplained icons.
